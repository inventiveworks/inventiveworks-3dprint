We are a 3D printing business that specializes in creating prints and patterns specifically for each unique client. By layer-by-layer extruding filament through a heated nozzle, we develop a 3-dimensional computer-generated design to make these one-of-a-kind items. In order to create the required final result, the heated nozzle softens and deposits heated material at predetermined spots on the printer bed.
